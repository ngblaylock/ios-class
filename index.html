How to save these PDF files to iBooks

  1. Tap on the image below of the file you want to save to ibooks.
  2. When the new tab loads up with the PDF, tap anywhere on the screen and you will see that the option "Save to iBooks" appears near the top right corner of the page.
  3. Tap "Save to iBooks" and you will be redirected to the iBooks app. The PDF will then appear on your device. You do not need to be connected to the internet to read the PDF document.
